How To Play

In the game, you will be shown four pictures of different video games. You must select the correct name of the game from the four options given.

If you choose the wrong game, you will lose a life. You have three lives in total, If you lose all three lives, the game is over

There are 10 rounds in total

The object of the game is to correctly identify as many games as possible and get the highest score possible.
